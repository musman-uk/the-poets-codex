Purpose

The Poets Codex is a quiet manuscript that gathers poetic voices, cultural lineages, and the landscapes that shaped them. It is a personal archive that grows slowly and intentionally.

Structure

The Codex is divided into sections that explore different dimensions of poetic identity. Each section will expand over time as new material is added.
