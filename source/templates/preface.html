Preface

Welcome to all. This codex is more metaphysical than literal, a place where poets who never shared a moment in history can sit together for a while. Each poet lived in a different world, shaped by different landscapes, languages, and beliefs. Yet their words seem to recognise one another, and I have always felt that although they were separated by time and distance, they can be brought together here, as if they are in a room, and we can be the ones walking into it and inhabiting it with them.

Mohammed Usman
