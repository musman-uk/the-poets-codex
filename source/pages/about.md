**The Poets Codex** is an ongoing project that brings together a small, intentional collection of poets.

Rather than building an exhaustive archive, the Codex focuses on depth over breadth – presenting each poet with a dedicated page, a distinct palette, and a short introduction to their work and influence.

The project is handcrafted using a custom static‑site generator, with each page assembled from Markdown and HTML templates.  
It is designed to feel calm, minimal, and manuscript‑like in tone.
